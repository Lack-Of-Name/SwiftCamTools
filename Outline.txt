SwiftCamTools is a camera app designed for IOS, specificially low-light conditions. It has a minimalist desgn, similar to that of the default IOS camera app that makes it easy for first-timers to use. As well as being easy to use, it has more advanced options available.

Tech stack:

Capture long-exposure frames    ->	AVCaptureDevice + AVCapturePhotoOutput
Capture multiple exposures (bracketed)  ->  AVCapturePhotoBracketSettings
RAW sensor data ->  AVCapturePhotoOutput with .unprocessed
Noise reduction + fusion    ->  Apple's built-in fusion OR custom Metal pipeline
UI  ->  SwiftUI or UIKit
Performance ->  Metal + Accelerate
